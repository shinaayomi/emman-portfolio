Who We are?

House Of Miss OGA - HOMO African fashion agency that blends the cultural and traditional fashion trend of the African continent with the exotic look and contempoary design asthetic of the modern western fashion.
We were founded April 2016 and have continued to create unique designs that mixed the best of African and American culture. All of our clothes, bags and shoes are proudly manufactured in Nigeria and shipped worldwide on request.
We delight our customers with exquisite, top notch, contemporary African fashion.

  • Quality Assurance
  • Up-to-date design trends
  • Craftsmanship
  • Professional Manufacture to Client relationship
  • Incredible design

Why Choose HOMO

our dedication to creating beautiful and stunning designs is at the core of our identity. It guides our day-to-day design strategies while inspiring us to think big for the long term. It reflects what makes our end product so special and our commitment to giving the world a positive view of the African style.
